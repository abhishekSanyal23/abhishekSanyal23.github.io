Private Data Blindness

LLMs have no access to your organization's internal, proprietary knowledge bases.

The Solution: An Open-Book Exam for AI

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) transforms the "closed-book" LLM into an "open-book" system. It connects the AI to external, authoritative knowledge bases in real-time, grounding its answers in verifiable facts.

Drastically Reduced Hallucinations

By grounding responses in real data, RAG significantly improves factual accuracy.

Always-Current Information

RAG provides the LLM with the latest information from your data sources for relevant answers.

Enhanced Trust & Transparency

Systems can cite their sources, allowing users to verify the information and trust the output.

RAG vs. Fine-Tuning: What's the Difference?

They solve different problems but can be used together for optimal results.
